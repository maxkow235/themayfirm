California’s Premiere Personal Injury Law Firm

The May Firm is a personal injury law firm. This means we only represent injured people and never insurance companies. This focus gives our firm an edge on other law firms that attempt to be experts in various areas of the law and our track record proves this. We have won several multi-million dollar cases and have recovered tens of millions dollars on behalf of our clients. We know what we are doing. With our impressive record and so many satisfied clients, many of our new clients are referrals from previous clients and other attorneys.

We are accident lawyers. We are well known and respected for fighting tirelessly for our clients while simultaneously alleviating as much of the burden from our clients’ shoulders as possible. This gives clients the freedom to focus on recovering from their injuries and emotional trauma without having to fight a legal battle alone. We have a reputation for taking on difficult cases that other firms turn away.

Compassion, Experience, Results

Each client’s case is handled with the utmost attention to detail. Every effort is made to ensure their clients receive maximum compensation under the law. All of use at The May Firm do all we can to ensure our clients’ immediate needs are taken care of throughout the process.

When someone suffers a personal injury, they are at their most vulnerable and insurance companies will try to take advantage. This is why accident injury victims need the guidance and support of professionals who care and have the experience to win. We at The May Firm serve as powerful advocates for injured persons and their families. Call for a free case consultation, we are here to help.
